Using this application, insert the photo of your exotic plant, stored on your computer, at it's location on the world map. If you do not know its name, others will help identify it. Also draw shapes on the world map to customize your map showing details of its location. Shapes are: circles, ellipses, rectangles, text, paths, and polygons.
After you have added your photo and location details, select 'Add Your Plant Map' button.
Viewers of your map can contact you via your email address for questions or answers about your exotic plant.
Markers showing map locations. Click on a marker to show its photo, map number, and title. Then click photo to preview this plant's map. A red marker indicates help is needed to identify the plant.
Drawing SVG Shape Elements On World Map

SVG shapes are: Circles, ellipses, rectangles, text, paths, polygons, and images.

These shapes can be added to your map to pinpoint plant locations.

Paths - Both linear and curved paths can be created by clicking their points on the drawing. Drag any point to modify its shape.

GPS Locator

You can use a GPS unit to precisely locate your plant.

Select the checkbox to display the GPS crosshairs. You can move the cursor to the desired location, then right-click mouse button to fix its GPS value and center the map initially at the GPS point.

Also, you may insert a GPS value and select its associated 'go' button to set the crosshairs and center the drawing at the GPS point.

Note: If crosshairs are set when you select 'Add Your Plant Map' button, the GPS value will be included in your map's commentary.
